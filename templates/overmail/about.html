About Assitant Overmail

Assistant Overmail allows you to set up an GPT-4 email assistant with minimal fuss. No sign up required.

You can pay in fiat via stripe or via Bitcoin lightning to preserve your privacy & save on processing fees.

How it works

  1. A lightning or stripe invoice is generated with an email address attached to it.
  2. Once paid, the email address is added to a list that the GPT-4 email assistant would reply to.
  3. Every $20 paid adds 200 emails to the email's quota.
  4. Simply send an email to {{GPT4_EMAIL}} with your instruction or prompt. A few seconds later, you would receive a response.
